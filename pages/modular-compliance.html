Modular Compliance

AI-Powered Regulatory Compliance Automation

Modular Compliance transforms how organisations manage regulatory obligations. Our platform uses advanced AI to analyse regulatory documents, identify compliance gaps, generate automated reports, and keep your compliance team ahead of changing requirements.

The Problem

Compliance Is Broken

Regulatory compliance in financial services, healthcare, and government is overwhelmingly manual. Compliance teams spend thousands of hours reviewing documents, cross-referencing requirements, and producing reports that are outdated the moment they're completed. As regulations multiply and change faster than ever, traditional approaches simply cannot keep up.

The Solution

Intelligent Automation for Every Stage

Modular Compliance handles the entire compliance lifecycle, from document ingestion to continuous monitoring.

Regulatory Document Analysis

Automatically process and interpret regulatory documents, extracting obligations, requirements, and key clauses. No more manual reading through hundreds of pages.

Compliance Gap Detection

Compare your current policies and controls against regulatory requirements to identify gaps, risks, and areas requiring attention — in minutes, not months.

Automated Reporting

Generate compliance reports, audit trails, and management summaries automatically. Always up-to-date, always audit-ready.

Continuous Monitoring

Stay ahead of regulatory changes with intelligent monitoring that alerts your team to new or amended requirements relevant to your organisation.
